Meditation Tips

  • Find a quiet and comfortable space for your practice.
  • Start with just 5-10 minutes a day and gradually increase the duration.
  • Focus on your breathing to bring your attention to the present moment.
  • Be consistent with your practice for better results.
  • Don’t judge your thoughts—just observe and let them pass.
  • Use the 4-7-8 Breathing Technique: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7 seconds, exhale for 8 seconds.
  • Practice Lotus Pose for better posture: Sit cross-legged with your feet on opposite thighs, keeping your back straight.
