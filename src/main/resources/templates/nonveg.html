Non-Vegetarian Diet Plan

Discover a balanced non-vegetarian diet plan with a variety of meals rich in protein and essential nutrients.

Sample Meal Plan

Breakfast

  • Scrambled eggs with whole-grain toast and avocado
  • Grilled chicken sandwich
  • Omelette with spinach and mushrooms

Lunch

  • Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and vinaigrette
  • Baked salmon with quinoa and roasted vegetables
  • Chicken curry with steamed rice and cucumber salad

Snack

  • Boiled eggs with a dash of paprika
  • Greek yogurt with a handful of nuts
  • Tuna salad on crackers

Dinner

  • Grilled shrimp skewers with a side of stir-fried vegetables
  • Roast chicken with sweet potato and broccoli
  • Fish tacos with a fresh salsa topping

Tips for a Healthy Non-Vegetarian Diet

  • Focus on lean protein sources like chicken, turkey, and fish.
  • Incorporate a variety of colorful vegetables in every meal.
  • Limit processed meats and fried foods.
  • Include omega-3-rich seafood like salmon and mackerel.
  • Stay hydrated and pair meals with healthy carbohydrates like quinoa or brown rice.
