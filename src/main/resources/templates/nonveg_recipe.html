Discover a balanced non-vegetarian diet plan that includes essential proteins and nutrients to keep you healthy.

High-Protein Chicken Salad

Description: A refreshing salad with grilled chicken, leafy greens, and a tangy vinaigrette.

Ingredients:

  • 150g grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation:

Combine all ingredients in a bowl, toss with olive oil and lemon juice, and serve fresh.

Grilled Salmon with Vegetables

Description: A nutrient-rich meal featuring omega-3-packed salmon with grilled vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 200g salmon fillet
  • 1/2 cup zucchini, sliced
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation:

Marinate salmon and vegetables in olive oil and spices, then grill for 10-15 minutes.

Egg and Avocado Toast

Description: A quick and nutritious breakfast packed with healthy fats and proteins.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 boiled or poached eggs
  • Salt, pepper, and chili flakes to taste

Preparation:

Spread mashed avocado on toast, top with sliced eggs, and season with salt and chili flakes.

Grilled Chicken with Quinoa Salad

Description: A balanced meal featuring lean grilled chicken served with a nutritious quinoa salad.

Ingredients:

  • 200g grilled chicken breast
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation:

Combine quinoa, cucumber, and bell pepper in a bowl. Drizzle with olive oil, lemon juice, and season with salt and pepper. Serve with grilled chicken.

Turkey and Avocado Wrap

Description: A healthy wrap featuring lean turkey and creamy avocado for a fulfilling snack or lunch.

Ingredients:

  • 100g sliced turkey breast

Preparation:

Spread mustard on the tortilla, layer turkey, avocado slices, and mixed greens. Roll up the tortilla and cut in half to serve.

Spicy Shrimp Tacos

Description: Flavorful shrimp tacos with a spicy kick, served with a side of crunchy slaw.

Ingredients:

  • 200g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 small corn tortillas
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation:

Season shrimp with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook in a skillet until pink. Serve on tortillas with shredded cabbage and a squeeze of lime.

Chicken Fajita Recipe

Description: A flavorful and spicy dish that combines the goodness of chicken, bell peppers, onions, and a savory sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 large bell peppers (any color), sliced
  • 8 small flour tortillas

Preparation:

Marinate the chicken in lime juice, olive oil, garlic, oregano, cumin, and cayenne pepper for at least 30 minutes. Grill or cook the chicken and vegetables in a skillet until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender. Serve with warm flour tortillas.

Lentil and Chicken Soup

Description: A hearty and comforting soup made with lentils and chicken for a nutritious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 200g chicken breast, shredded
  • 1 cup lentils, rinsed
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation:

Cook the chicken and vegetables in a pot. Add lentils and chicken broth. Simmer for 25-30 minutes until lentils are tender. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Vegetable Stir-Fry with Tofu

Description: A delicious stir-fry made with tofu and a variety of colorful vegetables, perfect for a quick and healthy meal.

Ingredients:

  • 200g tofu, cubed
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1/2 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated

Preparation:

Heat sesame oil in a pan, stir-fry tofu until golden. Add vegetables and ginger, and stir-fry until tender. Drizzle with soy sauce and serve.
